Welcome to The Career Navigator!

This platform is designed to help students explore various majors and make informed decisions about their academic journey. Through personalized assessments, employee blogs, and multimedia resources, we provide a comprehensive overview of each major.

Why Choose The Career Navigator?

  • Interactive personality tests tailored to suggest majors.
  • Access to employee blogs sharing real-world experiences.
  • Short videos and podcasts featuring career experts.
  • Information on courses, networks, and top universities.

Our Mission

We aim to empower first-year students by providing unbiased guidance that considers their interests and career aspirations, helping them navigate their choices confidently.
